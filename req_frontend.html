Frontend Engineer

Do you want to be part of an exciting & historically-significant product that millions of users use everyday? The Yahoo.com team is looking for an experienced, self-motivated, and passionate application engineer to work on building the presentation layer for advanced user interfaces. You'll work with product managers, designers, researchers, and marketers in a highly collaborative environment where everyone helps to define the product vision.

The ideal candidate will have:
* At least 7+ years of work experience in software development
* At least 4+ years of work experience in web application development
* Extensive experience in DHTML (JavaScript 1.0-1.5, CSS1 & CSS2, DOM Levels 0-2)
* Extensive knowledge of AJAX concepts (XmlHTTPRequest, iframes, remote scripting, JSON, etc)
* Extensive knowledge of cross-browser compatibility & browser degradation strategies
* Proven ability to design and architect complex applications involving extensive UI and application logic
* Proven leadership qualities and ability to push a new project forward
* Previous work experience with C++, C#, Java, or other similar programming languages
* Previous work experience with PHP, JSP, ASP, or other similar scripting languages
* Deep understanding of OOP and event-driven programming
* Knowledge of HCI and application UI design
* Knowledge of web services (SOAP, REST, XML-RPC, etc)
* BSCS/MSCS or equivalent work experience

You should be able to personally demonstrate your abilities with demos, blogs, web sites, articles, or anything else that would establish your expertise with these technologies.

If you're interested, please provide URLs and code samples of your work. Having your own site is a bonus!
