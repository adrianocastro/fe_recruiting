Frontend Engineer for Communities

We're looking for an experienced, self-motivated, and passionate frontend engineer to join the Communities Engineering department. You'll work closely with software engineers, product managers, interaction & visual designers, user experience researchers, and marketers on our growing Communities department, the team that brought you Yahoo! 360, Groups, and other community-based offerings.

The ideal candidate will have a strong command of PHP, object-oriented design, dynamic content retrieval, performance optimization techniques, unit testing techniques, debugging techniques, state management, HTTP, and Apache. Experience with client-side code (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, DHTML), cross-browser compatibility issues, working with a Unix environment, CVS, and bug tracking tools is highly desirable. A Computer Science degree or related work experience is also required.

The environment is fast-paced with numerous user-centered iterative design & development cycles. The web is a great medium for building fun and effective communities; here, you'll have a chance to join a growing team who's committed to crafting the best experience for our customers.

If you're interested, please provide URLs and code samples of your work. Having your own site is a bonus!
