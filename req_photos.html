Frontend Engineer for Yahoo! Photos

We're looking for an experienced, self-motivated, and passionate frontend engineer to join the Communities Engineering department. You'll work closely with software engineers, product managers, interaction & visual designers, user experience researchers, and marketers on our growing Photos team.

The ideal candidate will have a strong command of JavaScript, DHTML, DOM Levels 0-2, CSS, PHP, cross-browser compatibility issues, browser degradation strategies, optimization techniques (both in file weight and perceived/actual rendering speed), accessibility techniques, device-portability techniques, and internationalization issues. A Computer Science degree or related work experience is also required.

Experience working with a Unix environment, CVS, and bug tracking tools is highly desirable. Knowledge of remote scripting, state management, Apache, and HTTP is helpful.

The environment is fast-paced with numerous user-centered iterative design & development cycles. The web is a great medium for building photos; here, you'll have a chance to join a growing team who's committed to crafting the best experience for our customers.

If you're interested, please provide URLs and code samples of your work. Having your own site is a bonus!
